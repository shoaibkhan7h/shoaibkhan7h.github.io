About the Scheme

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS) encourages meritorious students from economically weaker sections to continue their education at secondary level.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class 8 with good academic performance
  • Family income below ₹3.5 lakh per annum
  • Studying in government or aided schools
  • Regular attendance required
  • Selection through examination

Key Benefits

  • ₹12,000 scholarship per year
  • Financial support from Class 9 to 12
  • Encourages merit-based education
  • Reduces dropout rate
  • Supports weaker sections
