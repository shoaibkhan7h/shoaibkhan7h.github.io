About the Scheme

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) is a national initiative aimed at improving the Child Sex Ratio and promoting education, survival, and empowerment of the girl child across India.

Key Objectives

  • Prevent gender-biased sex selection
  • Improve Child Sex Ratio
  • Ensure survival & protection of the girl child
  • Promote education of girls
  • Encourage girl child enrollment in schools
  • Change societal mindset
  • Ensure equal rights for women
  • Empower women socially & economically

Who Can Benefit

  • Girl children
  • Women and mothers
  • Families across rural & urban India
