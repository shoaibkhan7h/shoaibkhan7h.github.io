About the Scheme

This scheme provides safe, secure, and affordable hostel facilities for working women, students, and trainees living away from home.

Key Benefits

  • Safe living environment
  • Affordable accommodation
  • Day-care facilities for children
  • Security arrangements
  • Urban & semi-urban coverage
  • Support for working mothers
  • Basic amenities provided
  • Women empowerment

Eligibility

  • Working women
  • Students & trainees
