About the Scheme

PMMVY is a maternity benefit programme providing financial assistance to pregnant and lactating women for safe delivery and nutrition.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant women above 19 years
  • First live birth

Key Benefits

  • Cash assistance of ₹5,000
  • Amount paid in installments
  • Improved maternal nutrition
  • Encourages institutional delivery
  • Reduces maternal mortality rate
  • Regular health monitoring
  • Support during pregnancy period
  • Overall women welfare & empowerment

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account
  • Pregnancy Certificate
