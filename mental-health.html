About the Scheme

The National Mental Health Programme (NMHP) focuses on providing accessible, affordable, and quality mental healthcare services across India. It aims to integrate mental health with general healthcare services.

Objectives

  • Promote mental well-being
  • Early detection and treatment of mental illness
  • Reduce stigma related to mental health
  • Strengthen mental health institutions
  • Community-based mental healthcare

Benefits

  • Free counselling services
  • Psychiatric treatment at government hospitals
  • Mental health awareness programs
  • Rehabilitation and follow-up support
  • Tele Mental Health services

Eligibility

All Indian citizens are eligible under this programme.
