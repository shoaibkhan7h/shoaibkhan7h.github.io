About the Scheme

Janani Suraksha Yojana aims to reduce maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional delivery among pregnant women.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant women from BPL families
  • Women above 19 years
  • Applicable in rural and urban areas

Benefits

  • Cash assistance for institutional delivery
  • Free delivery and medicines
  • Support during pregnancy
