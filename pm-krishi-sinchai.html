About the Scheme

PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana) aims to enhance irrigation coverage, improve water use efficiency, and ensure water availability to every farm in India.

Objectives

  • Expand irrigation coverage through new irrigation projects
  • Improve water use efficiency and promote micro-irrigation
  • Reduce water wastage in agriculture
  • Enhance soil health and water conservation practices
  • Ensure equitable water distribution for small & marginal farmers
  • Encourage rainwater harvesting and watershed development
  • Promote participatory irrigation management among farmers
  • Strengthen agricultural productivity and income

Eligibility

All farmers across India are eligible, including small and marginal farmers.

Key Benefits

  • Improved irrigation facilities leading to higher crop yield
  • Reduced dependency on monsoon rains
  • Financial support for installing micro-irrigation systems
  • Efficient water management at farm level
  • Access to modern irrigation technology
  • Promotion of sustainable farming practices
  • Support for watershed and water conservation projects
  • Better income and livelihood security for farmers
