About the Scheme

The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is a digital platform launched by the Government of India to provide financial assistance to meritorious and economically weaker students across the country. It acts as a single-window system for all central and state government scholarships.

Key Objectives

  • Ensure transparency in scholarship disbursement
  • Provide timely financial support to students
  • Reduce duplication and fraud
  • Promote digital and cashless transactions
  • Encourage higher education among disadvantaged groups

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Family income below ₹2.5 lakh per annum
  • Students from SC, ST, OBC, Minority and EWS categories
  • Valid caste / community certificate
  • Enrolled in a recognized school or college

Scholarship Benefits

  • Financial support for school and college education
  • Tuition fee, maintenance allowance and other benefits
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to bank account
  • Coverage for both pre-matric and post-matric levels
  • Merit-based and need-based scholarships

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Bonafide / Admission Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Caste / Community Certificate (if applicable)

Application Process

  • Register on National Scholarship Portal
  • Fill online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Institute and state verification
  • Scholarship amount credited via DBT
