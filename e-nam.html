About the Platform

e-NAM is a digital trading platform that connects farmers, traders and buyers across India for transparent pricing.

Key Features

  • Online bidding system
  • Transparent price discovery
  • Pan-India market access
  • Reduced intermediaries
  • Quality assaying support
  • Better farmer income
  • Real-time market data
  • Digital payments

Who Can Use

  • Farmers
  • Traders
  • FPOs
