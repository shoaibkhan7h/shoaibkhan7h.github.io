About the Scheme

The Prime Minister Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) is a welfare initiative by the Government of India to encourage higher technical and professional education for the wards and widows of Ex-Servicemen and Ex-Coast Guard personnel.

Objectives

  • Promote higher education among defence families
  • Provide financial assistance for professional courses
  • Support wards of martyrs and ex-servicemen
  • Reduce financial burden on defence families
  • Encourage merit-based education

Eligibility Criteria

  • Ward / widow of ex-servicemen or ex-coast guard personnel
  • Minimum 60% marks in last qualifying examination
  • Admission in recognized professional courses
  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Valid service-related documents required

Scholarship Benefits

  • ₹2,500 per month for boys
  • ₹3,000 per month for girls
  • Scholarship for full academic year
  • Direct Benefit Transfer to bank account
  • Supports technical and professional education

Courses Covered

  • Engineering
  • Medical and Dental
  • MBA / MCA
  • Pharmacy
  • Other approved professional courses

Application Process

  • Apply through National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
  • Fill online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Verification by institute and authorities
  • Scholarship credited via DBT
