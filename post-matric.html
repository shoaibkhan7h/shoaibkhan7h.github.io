About the Scheme

Post Matric Scholarship is provided to students belonging to SC, ST and OBC categories pursuing education after Class 10 to reduce financial burden and promote higher education.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Student must belong to SC / ST / OBC category
  • Studying in Class 11 or above
  • Family income as per category norms
  • Admission in recognized institution
  • Regular attendance required

Scholarship Benefits

  • Tuition fee reimbursement
  • Maintenance allowance
  • Support for higher education
  • Encourages continuation of studies
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
