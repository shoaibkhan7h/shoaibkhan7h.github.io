About the Scheme

PMFBY provides insurance coverage to farmers against crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Premium Rates

  • Kharif crops – 2%
  • Rabi crops – 1.5%
  • Commercial crops – 5%

Key Benefits

  • Protection against crop loss
  • Low premium burden
  • Government subsidy support
  • Quick claim settlement
  • Weather risk coverage
  • Income stability for farmers
  • Use of technology for assessment
  • Encourages risk-free farming
