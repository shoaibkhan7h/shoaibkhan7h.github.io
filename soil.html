About the Scheme

Soil Health Card provides farmers with detailed soil analysis to improve productivity and reduce fertilizer misuse.

Key Benefits

  • Improved soil fertility
  • Balanced fertilizer usage
  • Higher crop yield
  • Reduced input costs
  • Scientific soil testing
  • Eco-friendly farming
  • Better nutrient management
  • Long-term soil health

Services Provided

  • Soil testing
  • Crop-wise recommendations
