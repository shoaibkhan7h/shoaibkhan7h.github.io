About the Scheme

Mahila Shakti Kendra aims to empower rural women through community participation, awareness generation, and access to government services.

Key Services

  • Skill development training
  • Employment & entrepreneurship support
  • Digital literacy programs
  • Legal awareness
  • Health & nutrition awareness
  • Support for self-help groups
  • Financial inclusion guidance
  • Leadership development

Target Group

  • Rural women
  • Marginalized communities
