About the Scheme

Pre Matric Minority Scholarship is a Government of India initiative to encourage education among students belonging to notified minority communities studying from Class 1 to Class 10.

Eligible Minority Communities

  • Muslim
  • Christian
  • Sikh
  • Buddhist
  • Jain
  • Parsi

Eligibility Criteria

  • Student must belong to a minority community
  • Studying in Class 1 to Class 10
  • Annual family income below ₹1,00,000
  • Regular student in a recognized school
  • Minimum attendance as prescribed by school

Scholarship Benefits

  • Admission fee support
  • Tuition fee assistance
  • Maintenance allowance
  • Reduces dropout rate among minority students
  • Encourages continuation of school education

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Minority Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • School Bonafide Certificate
