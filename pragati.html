About the Scheme

AICTE Pragati Scholarship is aimed at supporting girl students pursuing technical education by providing financial assistance and encouraging women empowerment.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Girl student only
  • Admission in AICTE approved institute
  • Pursuing diploma or degree course
  • Family income limit as prescribed
  • Indian citizen

Scholarship Benefits

  • ₹50,000 per year scholarship
  • Support for tuition and study expenses
  • Encourages girls in technical fields
  • Promotes women empowerment
  • Direct Benefit Transfer
