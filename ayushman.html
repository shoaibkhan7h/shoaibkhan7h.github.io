About the Scheme

Ayushman Bharat Yojana (PM-JAY) is a flagship healthcare initiative of the Government of India that provides free and cashless medical treatment to poor and vulnerable families.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Families listed in SECC 2011 database
  • Economically weaker households
  • No income or age limit
  • Applicable for rural and urban beneficiaries
  • No enrollment fee required

Key Benefits

  • ₹5 lakh health cover per family per year
  • Cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals
  • Covers surgeries, diagnostics, medicines
  • Pre & post hospitalization expenses included
  • Portable across India

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card / SECC proof
  • Mobile Number
  • Ayushman Card (if available)
