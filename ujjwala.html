About the Scheme

PM Ujjwala Yojana provides LPG gas connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households to promote clean cooking fuel.

Eligibility

  • Woman applicant aged 18+
  • BPL household
  • No existing LPG connection

Key Benefits

  • Free LPG gas connection
  • Financial assistance for stove
  • Subsidized LPG cylinders
  • Reduction in indoor air pollution
  • Improved health of women
  • Time saving in cooking
  • Environment-friendly fuel
  • Empowerment of rural women

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Bank Account
