About the Scheme

Kisan Credit Card provides short-term credit to farmers for agricultural and allied activities at affordable rates.

Key Benefits

  • Low interest rate loans
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Crop insurance coverage
  • ATM-cum-debit card facility
  • Quick loan disbursement
  • Support for allied activities
  • Reduced dependency on moneylenders
  • Government-backed scheme

Eligibility

All farmers including tenant farmers and sharecroppers
