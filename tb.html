About the Program

This program aims to eliminate tuberculosis by providing early diagnosis, free treatment, and nutritional support to patients.

Key Objectives

  • Early TB detection
  • Free diagnosis and treatment
  • Prevent drug-resistant TB
  • Community awareness

Benefits

  • Free medicines
  • Nutrition support (Nikshay Poshan Yojana)
  • Monitoring and follow-up
