About the Mission

Mission Indradhanush aims to immunize all children and pregnant women against preventable diseases.

Target Group

  • Children below 2 years
  • Pregnant women
  • Remote and high-risk areas

Benefits

  • Free vaccination
  • Protection from life-threatening diseases
  • Improved child health
