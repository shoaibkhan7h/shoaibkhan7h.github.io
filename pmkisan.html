About the Scheme

PM-KISAN is a central sector scheme that provides income support to small and marginal farmers to meet agricultural and household needs.

Eligibility

  • Small and marginal farmers
  • Landholding farmer families
  • Indian citizens
  • Valid land ownership records

Key Benefits

  • ₹6,000 annual financial assistance
  • Paid in 3 equal installments
  • Direct Bank Transfer (DBT)
  • No middlemen involvement
  • Helps meet farming expenses
  • Supports rural income stability
  • Pan-India coverage
  • Government funded scheme

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land ownership documents
  • Bank account details
