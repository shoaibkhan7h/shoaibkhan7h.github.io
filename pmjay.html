Free & Cashless Health Coverage for Poor Families

Home Health

About the Scheme

PM Jan Arogya Yojana is the health insurance component of Ayushman Bharat. It provides financial protection to economically weaker families for secondary and tertiary hospitalization.

Eligibility

  • Families listed under SECC 2011
  • No age limit
  • No income limit
  • Rural and urban beneficiaries

Benefits

  • ₹5 lakh health cover per family per year
  • Cashless treatment
  • Coverage across India
  • Pre & post hospitalization included
